Netflix Roulette || Github Repo || Screenshots on Portfolio
Ever gotten home, sat down to watch Netflix, and after too much scrolling to find the -perfect- movie, realised that it was time for bed? This webpage allows users to randomly generate a number of movies and TV shows from Netflix.
More in-depth user control:

The application allows you to:
randomly shows you movies from your country's location upon loading up the webpage
shows movies from your past search history in a carousel wheel
upon searching, finds a number of movies according to your selection of number of, country and genre
creates a random button that allows you to randomly go to the webpage of a netflix movie from your selection if you still have trouble deciding
utilies a carousel wheel to display posters, ratings, a short synopsis and the movie names for your viewing pleasure

Business Traffic Tracker || Github Repo || Heroku Application
What is the hardest part about being a business owner? Knowing where to start. Most businesses rely on volume turnover, and our application aims to inform the user of current trends in vehicle and foot traffic in the sydney region.
More in-depth user control:

The application allows the user to:
click on the map a street where they would like to set up a business
name and save their search using mySQL, and select the type of business
analyse their saved searches
through analysing, they can see the foot and traffic data gathered by the government in a 5km surrounding radius to see what times traffic is high and whether the region is more reliant on foot or vehicle traffic for revenue
mobile responsive so data can still be viewed on small screens on-the-go
